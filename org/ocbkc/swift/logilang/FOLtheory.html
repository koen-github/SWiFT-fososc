class FOLtheory extends FOLutils with CTLbase

To do

Chide: For now FOLtheory is needed, to retrieve the constants and predicates. However, I think it is more elegant to differentiate between a theory and a language (as normally done in logic). This will also allow you to more naturally deal with Constants (they can be made an inner class to a language) and Predicates. The language will then contain the methods to retrieve and create predicates and constants, and they will be unique to that langauge. For example, it is then impossible that you have 2 predicates with the same name, but with a different arity. Read the book Logic & Structure of van Dalen again for inspiration. A disadvantage is that you cannot share predicates and constants between languages. Sometimes this can be handy, for example in EfeLang: they all share the big and fast predicate, while the constants may be different. Perhaps it is possible to build this in as an additional feature, that languages may share some constants or predicates. Another question is: should the bridge language be connected to the theory or to the language? I would say the latter.
